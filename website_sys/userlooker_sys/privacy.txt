================================================================================
                          USERLOOKER PRIVACY POLICY
================================================================================

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

Welcome to UserLooker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and 
protect information when you use our service.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We collect and process the following types of information:

A. Discord Data
   - Discord User IDs
   - Discord Usernames
   - Discord Nicknames (server-specific)
   - Message content from public Discord channels
   - Message timestamps

B. Roblox Data
   - Roblox Usernames (extracted from Discord nicknames)
   - Military rank information (as displayed in Discord nicknames)

C. Derived Information
   - First and last message dates
   - Total message counts
   - Number of servers (guilds) where a user was found
   - Rank change history

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. HOW WE COLLECT DATA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Data is collected through DiscordChatExporter (DCE) JSON exports of public 
Discord server channels. We do not:

   - Access private messages or DMs
   - Connect to Discord's API in real-time
   - Collect passwords or authentication tokens
   - Access any private or restricted channels

All data comes from publicly visible messages in Discord servers.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We use collected data for the following purposes:

   - Linking Discord accounts to Roblox usernames
   - Tracking military rank progression over time
   - Providing user lookup functionality
   - Maintaining historical records of user activity
   - Analyzing user patterns with AI (optional feature)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

   - Data is stored in MongoDB databases
   - Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
   - We implement reasonable security measures to protect data
   - Data is stored on secure servers

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. DATA RETENTION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

   - User data is retained indefinitely for historical tracking purposes
   - You may request deletion of your data (see Section 7)
   - Deleted data may persist in backups for up to 30 days

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. DATA SHARING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

   - When required by law or legal process
   - To protect our rights or safety
   - With your explicit consent

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. YOUR RIGHTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You have the right to:

   A. Access - Request a copy of your stored data
   B. Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
   C. Deletion - Request deletion of your data
   D. Objection - Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact us (see Section 10).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
8. COOKIES AND TRACKING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Our web application does NOT use:

   - Cookies
   - Tracking pixels
   - Analytics services
   - Third-party advertising

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Our service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly 
collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data 
from a child, please contact us immediately.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
